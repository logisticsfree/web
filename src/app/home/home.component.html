A place to manage all of your Logistics

Boombirds for logistics operations is a great tool to help you manage your field staff. Create workflows and trips, while effortlessly tracking and monitoring your fleet real-time. Boombirds simplifies your field operations with a host of features, including merging of trips and consignments, delivery route planning and optimisation etc. Manage trip schedules and deliveries using a smart Kanban board integrated with live maps and multidimensional filtering options to track what matters to your business.

Effectively manage logistics
operations

Create team-based task assignment allowing every member of the team to be self-organised and accountable. Leverage Kanban view to track status of tasks real-time.

Easily create policy driven
logistics workflows

Create customised workflows for all services and integrate your standard operating procedures, including checklists for every workflow step.

Convenient live trip
tracking & management

Create & track trips real-time. Combine multiple consignments into a single trip or merge trips to save time and cost. Increase efficiency by allowing drivers to change the route plan.

Plan your repeat
trips & tasks

Create recurring service requests for repeat trips and tasks. Clone service requests and schedule the same.

Flexible exception-based
trip management

Simple pay-out

Define & configure trip pay-outs. Set a payment range and additional surcharge amounts for services if required.
